SMS Verification

We'll send you a verification code to confirm your phone number

What SMS messages will you receive?

  • Account Verification: One-time verification codes to confirm your phone number and secure your account
  • Ride Notifications: Real-time updates about driver arrival, ride status, and trip confirmations
  • Important Updates: Critical notifications about schedule changes, cancellations, or service updates
  • Safety Alerts: Emergency notifications and safety-related communications

Message Frequency: You will receive verification codes immediately and ride notifications as scheduled. Message frequency varies based on ride activity.

Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe from SMS notifications. Reply HELP for assistance.
