Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how SkoolMotion ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our services (the "Service").

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make the site work properly and improve your experience.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for the Service to function (for example, security and basic site features).
  • Preferences Cookies: Remember your choices (for example, language or region) to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how the Service is used so we can improve performance and user experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: May be used to measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns or to show relevant messages (if enabled).

3. How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep the website working as expected (security, load balancing, session management).
  • Remember preferences and improve usability.
  • Measure site performance and fix issues.
  • Understand which pages are most helpful so we can improve content.

4. Managing your cookie choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See which cookies are stored and delete them.
  • Block third-party cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Block all cookies.

Please note: if you block or delete cookies, some parts of the Service may not work properly.

5. Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will post the updated policy on this page.
